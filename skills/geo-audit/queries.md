## Query selection guide for the competitive subagent

When selecting 10 queries for a specific company, prioritise:

1. **Always include** at least 2 category ownership queries most relevant to the company's primary positioning
2. **Always include** at least 2 versus queries featuring the company's main competitors
3. **Always include** at least 1 compliance query if the company targets regulated industries
4. **Always include** at least 1 problem-led query matching the company's core use case
5. Fill remaining slots with the most relevant queries from other categories

For example, for an insurance-focused automation vendor: prioritise queries 5, 16, 20, 23, 24, 26, 36 + relevant versus queries.
